Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Am I eligible to donate blood?

Eligibility typically requires you to be **over 18**, weigh at least **50 kg (110 lbs)**, and be in good general health. Restrictions may apply based on recent travel, medications, and certain health conditions. Please check our detailed donor guide for specifics.

How should I prepare for my donation?

The night before, get at least **8 hours of sleep**. On the day of, **eat a full, iron-rich meal** (avoid fatty foods) and **drink plenty of water** or juice. Bring a photo ID and your donor card if you have one.

How long does the donation process take?

The actual blood draw usually takes less than 15 minutes. However, the registration, medical screening, and post-donation recovery/refreshments will take a total time of about **45 minutes to one hour**.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Navigate to the Login page and click the "Forgot Password" link. You will be prompted to enter your registered email address. We will send you a **secure link to reset your password** instantly.

How often is the blood stock inventory updated?

The blood stock table on the homepage is updated in **real-time** as units are processed and distributed to hospitals. The "low-stock" alerts are active indicators of urgent needs.
